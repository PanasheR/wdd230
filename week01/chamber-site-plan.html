Site Purpose

The Hungwe Chamber Of Commerce is a board aimed at improving the socio-economic nature of the Ashdown Park, Harare Commuinty through SMEs funding, awreness campaigns, loans and annual general meeting. All members of the community are taken care of and their welfare is a priority

Scenarios

  • This years' major events?: We are glad to announce that this year we will be lauching our first community web based platfor for all community members to access from
  • Looking for contact info?: Those looking for the board's contact info can navigate to the site's home page or visit our nearest offices
  • Population Growth in the area?: 2022 had major peaks including population growth. Our community has grown from a population of 100 to 150 people. That's about 150%
